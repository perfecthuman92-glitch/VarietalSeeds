About Varietal Seeds

Founded in 2023, Varietal Seeds emerged with a strong commitment to empower Pakistan's farmers by providing high quality, locally adapted seed varieties and practical agronomic consultancy.

We recognized that while many farmers work tirelessly to feed the nation, they often face challenges like poor seed quality, unpredictable weather, pest infestations, and lack of proper technical guidance.

That's where Varietal Seeds steps in, bridging the gap between science and soil. Our company offers not just premium seed varieties but also expert advice tailored to each farmer's specific region, soil, and crop conditions.

Our Seeds

At Varietal Seeds, we believe that every successful harvest begins with the right seed. That's why we provide carefully selected, scientifically tested, and field proven seed varieties to meet the needs of farmers across Pakistan.

Wheat Varieties

Our wheat seeds are known for their high yield, drought tolerance, and resistance to rust diseases. Each variety is carefully adapted to local soil and climatic conditions.

  • Excellent grain quality and milling performance
  • Strong resistance against leaf and stem rust
  • Suitable for both irrigated and semi arid zones

Cotton Varieties

Our cotton seed varieties are designed to meet the modern challenges of pest pressure, water scarcity, and fiber quality.

  • High lint percentage and excellent fiber strength
  • Resistance to major insect pests
  • Early maturity to reduce exposure to late season pest attacks

Fodder Crops

Our fodder crop seeds are known for their fast growth, high nutritional content, and multi cut potential.

  • High protein and digestibility levels
  • Fast regrowth after each cut
  • Suitable for both irrigated and rain fed systems

Oilseed Crops

Our oilseed range provides farmers with profitable alternatives for crop rotation and soil improvement.

  • High oil yield and premium quality
  • Excellent resistance to pests and lodging
  • Improves soil fertility when rotated with cereal crops

Consultancy & Services

At Varietal Seeds, we believe that farming success depends not only on good seeds but also on the right guidance at every step of the crop journey.

1

Soil Testing and Fertility Management

Healthy soil is the foundation of a successful crop. We provide soil sampling, testing, and fertility assessment services to help farmers understand the nutrient status of their land.

2

Pest and Disease Management

Crop losses from pests and diseases can drastically reduce profits. Our consultants identify early symptoms and guide farmers on safe, effective control methods.

3

Weed Control Strategies

Weeds compete with crops for nutrients, water, and sunlight. We offer practical weed identification and control programs tailored to different crop types.

4

Irrigation Management

Water is one of the most valuable farm resources. We help farmers design and manage irrigation schedules based on soil type, crop stage, and weather conditions.

5

Crop Lifecycle Guidance

From sowing to harvest, our experts stay connected with farmers through every growth stage, providing timely advice on fertilization, irrigation, and pest control.

6

Yield Improvement and Farm Optimization

We help farmers unlock their field's full potential through yield analysis, field layout planning, and varietal recommendations suited to their region.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our seeds, services, and support.

What types of seeds does Varietal Seeds offer?

We offer premium quality seeds for Wheat, Cotton, Fodder, and Oilseed crops such as Canola, Super Raya, and Berseem. All our seeds are tested for germination, purity, and adaptability to local soil and climatic conditions.

How can I choose the right seed variety for my area?

Our agronomists and field experts can help you select the most suitable seed variety based on your soil type, water availability, and regional weather. You can contact us through phone, WhatsApp, or email for free guidance.

Are your seeds tested or certified for quality?

Yes. Every batch of Varietal Seeds undergoes strict quality testing and field trials before distribution. We ensure high germination rates, disease resistance, and consistent performance across various field conditions.

Do you provide consultancy services along with seed sales?

Absolutely! We believe in complete farmer support, not just selling seeds. Our consultancy services include soil testing, pest and weed management, irrigation planning, fertilizer guidance, and yield improvement strategies.

Can your team visit my farm to provide on ground advice?

Yes, our agronomists, extensionists, and botanists regularly conduct field visits in different areas to observe crop conditions and provide practical recommendations. You can also share photos or videos via WhatsApp for remote assistance.

What makes Varietal Seeds different from other suppliers?

We combine quality products with expert advice and easy accessibility. Our team is available throughout the crop cycle, offering practical, farmer focused support. We don't just sell seeds, we build long term partnerships for better yields.
